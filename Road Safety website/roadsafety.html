About Road Safety

Road safety is the measures taken to prevent road users from being killed or seriously injured. Typical road users include pedestrians, cyclists, motorists, vehicle passengers, horse-riders, and passengers of on-road public transport (mainly buses and trams).

Our Services

  • Traffic control and management
  • Road signage and markings
  • Vehicle and road design standards
  • Education and awareness campaigns
